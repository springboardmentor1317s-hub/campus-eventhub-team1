About this event

The annual Inter-College Debate Championship brings together the brightest minds from various institutions to engage in thought-provoking debates on current global issues. This year's theme is 'The Future of Technology and Society.'

Tags

Debate Academics Competition

Participation Requirements

Participants must be currently enrolled students at a recognized college or university. Teams should consist of two members. Prior debate experience is recommended but not mandatory.

Prizes

The winning team will receive a trophy, certificates, and a cash prize of $500. Runner-up teams will also receive certificates and smaller cash prizes.
